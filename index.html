About

I am a computational chemist working at the intersection of atomistic simulation and machine learning for chemistry and materials. My work focuses on building reliable models and workflows for interfacial chemistry and spectroscopy.

Research Focus

Machine Learning Potentials for Chemistry

  • Fourth-generation high-dimensional neural network potentials with charge equilibration.
  • Equivariant and graph-based models.
  • Physics-informed model training and validation.

Path-Integral MD and Vibrational Spectroscopy

  • Path Integral Molecular Dynamics (CMD, RPMD, and their successors) for nuclear quantum effects at interfaces and in condensed phases.
  • Vibrational spectra from time-correlation functions (IR, VSFG).

Materials Design and Informatics

  • Organic and inorganic materials design and discovery.
  • ML pipelines for crystal structure prediction and property prediction.
